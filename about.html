The Group

About

The Agentic Pedagogy Research Group studies what happens to teaching and learning when AI agents enter the classroom — as tutors, as tools, and as subjects of study themselves.

Mission

Why we exist

Agentic AI is arriving in education faster than the evidence about how to use it well. The group's job is to close that gap: we build agentic learning tools, deploy them with real teachers and students, and measure honestly what works, what doesn't, and for whom.

Everything we produce — research products, the PATHS program, our publications — is in service of one goal: making sure the people doing the teaching stay in charge of how machines join them.
